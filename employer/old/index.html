Our Services

For employers who need great people. Find Better, Faster.

Unlimited Job Postings

As an employer on our platform, you'll have the freedom to post an unlimited number of job listings, allowing you to find the perfect candidates for your diverse workforce needs.

Access To Diverse Candidates

Gain access to a rich pool of talented individuals from underrepresented communities, including women, persons with disabilities, LGBTQ individuals, veterans (Army, Navy, Airforce), and retirees. We connect you with the diverse talent across various industries in India.

Only D&I Job Portal in India

We are proud to be India’s exclusive platform dedicated to diversity and inclusion. By choosing us, you're joining a community committed to fostering an inclusive workplace culture.

Access to India’s Top HR Agencies

Collaborate with India's leading HR agencies to streamline your hiring process and access top-tier talent. Our partnerships across industries ensure you have the best candidates at your disposal.

Stay up-to-date with marketing trends

Stay ahead of the curve by staying informed about the latest trends in HR and recruitment. Access our daily blogs and updates on HR topics, market trends, and more to keep your hiring strategies current and effective.

Share Your Job Postings on Other Platforms

We provide you with the tools to amplify your recruitment efforts, increase visibility of your job listing and attract a broader range of applicants by easily sharing your job postings on other relevant platforms.
